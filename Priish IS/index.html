What we do ?



PRIISH, is for profit e-commerce company based in Bangalore. We are online sellers, selling on different platforms. When we started e-commerce business, we realised the huge opportunity for sellers. We tasted significant success and company grew a lot. At the same time, we also realised the difficulties sellers face. While we were getting good sales, many others were struggling. Even the sellers who get good sales are not able to manage the work load and time efficiency required by the super fast e- commerce industry. That time we decided to solve problems of all sellers. We are the only e-commerce company in India which is seller centric. We want to create an unmatched experience for sellers so that sellers/manufacturers/distributers can focus completely focus on their business and its growth.
